About Us

Mayuresh Super Specialty Hospital is situated in Navi Mumbai, a twin city of Mumbai. It is a 35 bedded Super Specialty Hospital, centrally located on Vashi Turbhe Road, easily accessible and reachable from Mumbai and Suburban Mumbai, it is in close proximity to Chembur & Thane City.


The Hospital is Centrally Air-conditioned and maintains utmost hygiene, ensuring safety and quality service to the patients.
